Fortnite

Fortnite, developed by Epic Games and released in 2017, is a highly popular online multiplayer game known for its multiple modes,
including Battle Royale, Save the World, and Creative. With over 350 million registered players as of 2021, Fortnite has made a significant cultural impact,
hosting virtual concerts with artists like Travis Scott and featuring crossovers with franchises such as Marvel and Star Wars.
The game is free-to-play but generates revenue through in-game purchases of V-Bucks for cosmetic items and battle passes.
Fortnite is renowned for its live in-game events, frequent updates, and support for cross-platform play
. Its competitive scene is vibrant, with the Fortnite World Cup 2019 boasting a $30 million prize pool.
The game has also popularized the battle royale genre and its iconic dances and emotes have permeated popular culture.
